# 🌤️ Online Weather Updates

**Get real-time weather information for any city at your fingertips.**

Welcome to Online Weather Updates, a simple and intuitive tool designed to keep you informed about current weather conditions. Whether you're planning your day or just curious about the weather in another part of the world, our app provides quick and accurate data.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Live Temperature:** Instantly see the current temperature in Celsius.
- **Weather Status:** Get a snapshot of the sky (e.g., Clear, Cloudy, Rain).
- **Humidity Tracking:** Check the moisture level in the air.
- **Wind Speed:** See how fast the wind is blowing in Km/h.
- **Smart Search:** Easily find weather details by entering any city name.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Enter Location:** Type the name of the city you want to check in the search bar.
2. **Get Updates:** Click the search icon or press Enter.
3. **View Details:** The app will display the current temperature, weather condition, humidity, and wind speed for that location.
